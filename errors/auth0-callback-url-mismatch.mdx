## Why this error occurs

This error occurs because `tunnelapp.dev` domains are not one of the domains whitelisted in your Auth0 Application Settings.

## Possible ways to fix it

Here are some possible fixes:

### Whitelist Tunnel URLs in your Auth0 Application Settings

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to your Auth0 Application Settings page">
    Click the **Application Settings page** link in the error message or navigate to the **[Auth0 Dashboard](https://manage.auth0.com/dashboard) > Applications > *Your Application* > Settings**.

    <Frame>
      <img src="assets/screenshots/errors/auth0-callback-url-mismatch/application-settings-link.png" />
    </Frame>

  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Tunnel URLs to your allowed URLs">
    Scroll to the **Application URIs** section. Add the following wildcard URL to both your **Allowed Callback URLs** and **Allowed Logout URLs**:

    ```
    https://*.tunnelapp.dev/*
    ```

    <Frame>
      <img src="assets/screenshots/errors/auth0-callback-url-mismatch/allowed-urls.png" />
    </Frame>

  </Step>
</Steps>
